About me

Hello, my name is Emile Hohnadel and this is my personal webpage.

I am a Postdoc in the ELAN team at INRIA Grenoble-Rhône-Alpes working with Florence Bertails-Descoubes and Thibaut Métivet on the physical simulations of fibrous materials in 2D and 3D. This is a continuation of my PhD thesis made under their supervision, which I defended in July 2025.

I am interested in developing efficient numerical modelling of complex physical and mechanical behaviours.
